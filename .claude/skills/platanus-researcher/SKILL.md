# Platanus Researcher Skill

Use this skill with the `researcher` agent.

## Procedure

1. Capture exact track wording before interpreting anything.
2. Research Platanus values, prior Hack projects, voting, judging hints, and winner lessons.
3. Research sponsor capabilities and constraints from official docs.
4. Mine YC/RFS and startups.rip for timing, wedge, and market lessons.
5. Generate 10 to 15 narrow product ideas.
6. Score ideas by track fit, pain, demo wow, feasibility, data availability, sponsor fit, team edge, and pitch clarity.
7. Recommend the top 3 with MVP scope and risks.

## Output

Return Markdown ready for `artifacts/01_research_brief.md`.

## Guardrails

- May write only `artifacts/01_research_brief.md`.
- Cite sources.
- Label uncertainty.
- Do not invent rules, APIs, credits, or sponsor access.
